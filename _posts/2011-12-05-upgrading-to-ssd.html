SSD = Solid State Drive

If you know anything about computers, you know that hard drives are moving the way of the SSD and the rest of your storage is going to the cloud (unless you need a ton of store for some reason).

The change is happening because of speed. Hard drives contain a lot of small moving parts which cause them to be slower by their very nature. Where as an SSD is more like the RAM that is in your computer. Wiki gives a good comparison of the two but the biggest drawback of an SSD is that it doesn't hold as much information.

Now, I could continue to go into great detail about the two however I'd rather just show you the performance increase I personally witnessed.
