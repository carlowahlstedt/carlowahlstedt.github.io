After getting our first full electric bill that equaled the bill from our other house (the new house has gas heat and the old one had electric), I decided I should look into the amount of money we're spending on lighting the house.

I quickly remembered a blog post from Scott Hanselman about the cost of lighting. I downloaded his template but since I don't use Microsoft Office (because I use Google Docs) I needed to convert it to a Google Spreadsheet. After doing that I decided to make it my own. After I did that, I decided to make it a Google Doc Template to share with the world. I feel like my previous template has been a relative success (1,422 users at current count). Enjoy!
